How can you help?

Your views matter, wherever you live now or dream of living in future. We really want to chat to you on a video call (we will send a Teams link) about houses, places and communities. This will help our clients make good decisions around planning, designing and/or developing those places.

Why your views are so important

Your time is valuable, and we want to ask you a range of questions about subjects that could include:

  • How you think about places (including where you’ve lived)?
  • What do you like and dislike about different places?
  • What do you value in a local community?
  • What sort of language do you use to describe different places?

Please be reassured that we are not trying to sell you anything. You don’t need to be considering a house move! We just want to learn from your insights and advise our clients on how people think about these important issues.

What happens if I decide to take part?

If you meet our criteria, we will send you an email inviting you to take part.

You can then book a convenient time to speak to our researcher. This will be on Microsoft Teams (we’ll send you a link).

Sessions will take up to 90 minutes. There is no preparation needed from you.

A thank you for your time

As a mark of gratitude, we would like to offer you £60 for completing the call. We will pay you within 5 working days of the session.
